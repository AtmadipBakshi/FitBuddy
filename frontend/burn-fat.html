🔥 Fat Loss Routine

Torch fat fast with HIIT and cardio-focused training.

💥 HIIT Circuit

  • Jump Squats – 30s
  • Mountain Climbers – 30s
  • Burpees – 30s
  • Rest – 1 min
  • Repeat – 3–5 rounds

🏃 Steady-State Options

  • 30–45 mins brisk walking
  • Cycling or Swimming
  • Incline Treadmill
📌 Tip: For optimal fat loss, combine this routine with a calorie-controlled diet.
