📈 Track Your Fitness Progress

Track smart. Stay accountable. Celebrate results.

📝 Things You Can Track

  • Body weight, BMI, body fat %
  • Strength (e.g., push-ups, squat reps)
  • Workout frequency & duration
  • Calories or macros (optional)
  • Energy, sleep, and mood

📒 Tools & Tips

Try these free tools to track your journey:

  • 🔗 MyFitnessPal – Log calories, macros, meals
  • 🔗 Strong App – Strength & gym tracking
  • 🔗 Fitbit – Steps, sleep, heart rate
  • 🔗 Google Fit – General fitness dashboard
  • 🔗 Apple Health – iPhone & Apple Watch users
📌 Tip: Progress takes time. Stay consistent and look for trends — not perfection.
