💪 Muscle Building Plan

Strength and size-focused workouts for consistent gains.

🔁 Weekly Split

  • Mon – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
  • Tue – Pull (Back, Biceps)
  • Wed – Legs
  • Thu – Rest
  • Fri – Full Body
  • Sat – Arms & Core
  • Sun – Active Recovery

🏋️‍♂️ Key Exercises

  • Bench Press – 4x8
  • Barbell Squats – 4x10
  • Deadlifts – 3x6
  • Overhead Press – 3x10
  • Lat Pulldown – 3x12
📌 Tip: Focus on progressive overload and track your lifts weekly.
