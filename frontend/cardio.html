❤️ Cardio Endurance Plan

Boost your stamina with structured weekly cardio routines.

📅 Weekly Schedule

  • Mon – 20min Jog
  • Wed – Interval Cycling
  • Fri – Rowing Machine
  • Sun – Long Walk (5km+)

🔍 Heart Rate Zones

  • Zone 2 – Endurance (60–70%)
  • Zone 3 – Aerobic (70–80%)
  • Zone 4 – Threshold (80–90%)
📌 Tip: Consistency builds endurance. Don’t skip your recovery days.
