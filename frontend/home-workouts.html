🏠 Home Workout Plan

No equipment? No problem. Bodyweight workouts for anytime, anywhere.

🚶 Beginner Routine

  • Push-ups – 3x10
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3x15
  • Plank – 3x30s
  • Glute Bridges – 3x12

🔥 Intermediate Circuit

  • Jump Lunges – 3x12
  • Burpees – 3x10
  • Wall Sit – 3x45s
  • Mountain Climbers – 3x30s
📌 Tip: Do 3–4 rounds. Rest 30s between moves. Stay hydrated!
